Last Updated: April 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Chat AI Pro Trading Analysis ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application available on the Google Play Store.

We collect only limited information necessary to provide our services:

Images uploaded by users are processed temporarily to generate AI-based trading insights.
